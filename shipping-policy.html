Shipping Policy

Thank you for visiting and shopping at VKclothing. Following are the terms and conditions that constitute our Shipping Policy.

Shipment Processing Time

All orders are processed within **1-3 business days**. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Currently, we are offering **FREE promotional delivery** on all orders across India. (Standard delivery charge of ₹49 is waived until further notice).

  • Estimated Delivery Time: 3-7 business days depending on your location in India.

Delivery delays can occasionally occur due to courier peak seasons or weather conditions.

Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking

You will receive a shipment confirmation message on WhatsApp once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.

Customs, Duties and Taxes

VKclothing is not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.).

Damages

VKclothing is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.

International Shipping

We currently only ship to addresses within **India**.
