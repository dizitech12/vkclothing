Privacy Policy

At VKclothing, accessible from VKclothing.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by VKclothing and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or create an account, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To address you and process the order.
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding order updates and delivery.
  • Shipping Address: To deliver your products to your doorstep.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website.
  • Process your transactions and manage your orders.
  • Communicate with you via WhatsApp or phone for order confirmations and tracking.
  • Find and prevent fraud.

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Your contact information is stored securely and used only for business purposes related to VKclothing.

Payment Information

All payments are handled via secure third-party UPI providers or Cash on Delivery. We do not store your credit card or bank details on our servers.

Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at vkclothing111@gmail.com.
