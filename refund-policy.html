Refund Policy

At VKclothing, we strive to provide the best quality products and service. Please read our refund and cancellation policy carefully before making a purchase.

Cancellation Policy

Orders can only be cancelled **before they are shipped**. Once an order has been handed over to our courier partner and marked as "Shipped," cancellations are no longer possible.

Returns & Refunds

Due to the nature of our small business and promotional pricing, we currently follow a **No Return, No Refund** policy after the product has been shipped.

  • We do not accept returns if you change your mind.
  • We do not offer refunds once the order is in transit or delivered.
  • Currently, we do not support exchanges for size or color once the order is shipped. Please refer to our size guide carefully before ordering.

Payment Failures

If your payment fails but the money has been deducted from your account, please do not worry. This is usually a sync issue with the payment gateway. In such cases:

  1. Wait for 24-48 hours as the money is often automatically refunded by your bank.
  2. If the amount is not reversed, please contact our support team with your transaction details.
