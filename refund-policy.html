At VKclothing, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. Please read our refund and cancellation policy carefully before placing an order.

2. Refund Policy

  • Refunds are issued only in cases of:
    • Product is defective or damaged on delivery
    • Wrong product delivered (different from what was ordered)
    • Order cancelled before shipment (as per the cancellation table above)
    • Payment deducted but order not confirmed
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used during checkout.
  • Refund processing time: 5–7 business days after approval.
  • We do not accept returns for reasons such as size mismatch (please refer to the size chart before ordering), change of mind, or colour difference from screen.

3. Damaged or Wrong Product

If you receive a damaged or wrong item, please:

  • Take a clear photo/video at the time of unboxing
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Share the evidence via WhatsApp or email with your Order ID

Once verified, we will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost to you.

4. Payment Failure Refunds

If your payment was deducted but the order was not placed or confirmed, the amount will be automatically refunded to your source account within 5–7 business days. This is handled by Cashfree Payments.

For any payment-related issues, contact us at vkclothing111@gmail.com.

5. Non-Refundable Items

  • Products that have been used, washed, or altered
  • Products with removed tags or damaged packaging
  • Orders cancelled after shipment
  • Size/colour preference changes after delivery
